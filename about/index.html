Hey, Trey Manuszak here. Most know me for my willingness to open and explore the many doors that life throws at me. Below is a summary of those doors I've opened.

Programming

During my math undergrad, I took an elective "Intro to Programming" class during the Spring 2018 semester, which covered the basics of programming and the language Java. Just after quickly learning about reading console input, loops, and arrays, I started experimenting with my own programs. The first that I have record of, was a program that read an n-tuple of integers from the user and returned the repeating (or non-repeating, if n is a power of 2) sequence of n-tuples according to the rules of "the Diffy game".
